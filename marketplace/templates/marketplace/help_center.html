Frequently Asked Questions

Account & Profile

How do I create an account? +

Creating an account is easy! Click the "Sign Up" button in the top navigation, fill in your details, and verify your email address. You'll be ready to start bidding and selling in minutes.

How do I update my profile information? +

Go to your profile page and click "Edit Profile". You can update your personal information, contact details, and preferences at any time.

I forgot my password. How do I reset it? +

Click "Forgot Password" on the login page, enter your email address, and we'll send you a link to reset your password securely.

Bidding & Buying

How does bidding work? +

Enter your maximum bid amount, and our system will automatically bid on your behalf up to that amount. You'll be notified if someone outbids you.

Can I cancel my bid? +

Bids are binding and cannot be cancelled once placed. Please make sure you want to bid before placing your offer.

What happens if I win an auction? +

You'll receive an email notification and can complete your purchase through our secure payment system. The seller will ship your item once payment is confirmed.

Selling & Auctions

How do I create an auction? +

Click "Create Auction" in the navigation, upload photos of your item, write a detailed description, set your starting price and duration, and publish your listing.

What are the commission rates? +

Commission rates vary from 2-5% depending on your seller level and item value. Check our "Sell with Us" page for detailed commission information.

When do I receive payment? +

Payment is processed automatically once the buyer completes their purchase. You'll receive your funds within 3-5 business days after shipping confirmation.

Payments & Shipping

What payment methods do you accept? +

We accept all major credit cards, debit cards, UPI, net banking, and digital wallets. All payments are processed securely through our payment partners.

How is shipping handled? +

Sellers are responsible for shipping items to buyers. We provide shipping labels and tracking information to ensure smooth delivery.

What if my item arrives damaged? +

Contact our support team immediately with photos of the damage. We offer buyer protection and will help resolve the issue with the seller.

Security & Safety

Is my personal information secure? +

Yes, we use industry-standard encryption and security measures to protect your personal and financial information. We never share your data with third parties.

What if I encounter a fraudulent seller? +

Report suspicious activity immediately. We have a dedicated team that investigates fraud and takes appropriate action to protect our community.

Technical Issues

The website is not loading properly +

Try clearing your browser cache and cookies, or try a different browser. If the issue persists, contact our technical support team.

I'm not receiving email notifications +

Check your spam folder and ensure our email address is not blocked. You can also update your notification preferences in your account settings.
